## Key Insights from Research

### What Works on HN:
1. **Code samples above the fold** — Show, don't just tell
2. **Problem-first framing** — "I built this because..."
3. **Specific, concrete titles** — Avoid marketing speak
4. **Open source** — Strong preference for OSS projects
5. **Technical depth** — Don't be afraid of implementation details
6. **Humility** — Acknowledge alternatives and limitations

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid:
1. Marketing language ("revolutionary," "disruptive")
2. Vague claims without evidence
3. Defensive responses to criticism
4. Ignoring comments (HN algorithm penalizes this)
5. Posting at wrong times (weekends, late Friday)
